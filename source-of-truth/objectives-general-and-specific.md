# General and Specific Objectives

Aligned with the **relocation & investment facilitation** model described in the source-of-truth narrative (integrated “relocation system,” premium packages, European client focus, relational banking advantage).

---

## General objectives

1. **Integrate the journey**  
   Deliver a single, coherent path for legally establishing foreigners in Paraguay—residency, identification, banking, company and tax setup, and ongoing accounting—so clients avoid fragmented providers and failure modes.

2. **Professionalize trust**  
   Replace ad hoc, opaque intermediation with transparent packages, predictable timelines, and clear scope—positioning the business as a premium, execution-led alternative to a fragmented market.

3. **Compress time and uncertainty**  
   Turn multi-month friction (especially banking) into a managed process measured in days or a short visit where realistically achievable, without cutting legal corners.

4. **Scale client acquisition in Europe**  
   Build repeatable marketing and referral systems (web, social, network) with culturally aligned messaging—emphasizing opportunity, simplicity, and security rather than risky framing.

5. **Convert relational advantage into a system**  
   Codify workflows around banking and institutional relationships so growth does not depend only on informal memory, while preserving the strength of personal trust.

6. **Sustain recurring revenue**  
   Grow accounting, advisory, and investment-adjacent services so the business is not only project-based setup fees.

7. **Operate within clear ethical and regulatory boundaries**  
   Present services as legitimate optimization and compliance support; avoid gray-market perception and maintain reputational resilience.

## Relationship between general and specific

- **General objectives** answer *why* the project exists and *what* strategic direction to hold.  
- **Specific objectives** answer *what will be done* and *how we know it worked*—they should be reviewed yearly and tied to the business plan and risk register.
