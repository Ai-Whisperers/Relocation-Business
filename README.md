# Relocation-Business

Business planning repository for a professional European-to-Paraguay relocation and establishment service.

## What This Is

This repository contains the complete business documentation for a relocation company that helps European clients (primarily Dutch, German, and Scandinavian) establish legal, financial, and operational presence in Paraguay. It includes business plans, financial projections, marketing strategy, risk management, operational blueprints, and legal templates.

**Status:** Pre-launch planning phase. No application code exists yet.

## Repository Structure

| Directory | Contents |
|---|---|
| `documentos/` | Legal templates, service agreements, privacy policy, client guide, internal process manual |
| `marketing/` | Brand identity, marketing strategy, content plan, referral program, web content |
| `plan de negocios/` | Business plan, market analysis, financial projections, commercial strategy, organizational structure |
| `presupuesto/` | Budget: operational costs, revenue structure, initial investment, 3-year projections, marketing budget |
| `proyectos/` | Implementation roadmap, KPIs, website project plan, launch plan |
| `riesgos/` | Risk register, risk matrix, mitigation plan, continuity plan, compliance policy |
| `servicios/` | Service catalog, packages & pricing, operational blueprints, SLAs, client document checklists |
| `source-of-truth/` | Canonical reference material: original program description, questionnaires, conversation synthesis |

## Programs Offered

- **Paraguay Business** (USD 4,400) -- Residency, company incorporation, tax registration, bank account, one operational day
- **Paraguay Investor Program** (USD 6,900) -- All of the above plus 12 months accounting, legal consultation, and investment analysis
